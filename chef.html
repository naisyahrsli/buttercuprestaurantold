About

Welcome to Buttercup

Buttercup Restaurant is a cozy and cheerful dining place inspired by the warmth of the buttercup smell. We serve comforting dishes made with fresh ingredients and our signature buttery flavors, ranging from hearty meals to sweet desserts. With a soft and welcoming atmosphere, Buttercup is the perfect spot for families, friends, couples, and students to enjoy delicious food and relax together.
